File Sharing

Introduction History Methods Impact


File sharing is the practice of distributing or providing access to digitally stored information such as audio files, as well as video files, and other multi-media. Document files, electronic books and even computer programs can also be shared through the internet. There are various ways in which this can be achieved. Although File sharing is common practice today, it has not been without controversy particularly regarding the legal aspects of File Sharing.
